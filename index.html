GTCO ANNUAL FOOD AND DRINK FESTIVAL 2022

GTCO Food & Drink Fair is a an annual food exhibition and sales event aimed at projecting the diverse angles of the food industry.

2022.04.30 (Wed) ~ 2ND MAY (Sat)

@ GT Centre Plot 1, Water Corporation Drive, Oniru, VI, Lagos.

Main Program

  • Lecture

  • Listen to lectures from speakers from around the world and learn about the GTCO Food and Drink

  • Exhibition

  • Let's meet Chefs from various part of the country who share the open methods and techniques.

  • Kids Arena

  • An event for the kids. Children will have the opportunity to engage in fun and educational activities.

  • Classes

  • Fostering knowledge and capacity building for all. Featuring cooking lessons from renowed chefs.

  • Restuarant

  • Enjoy a variety of tastings from casual to gourmet traditional Nigerian and continental dishes
